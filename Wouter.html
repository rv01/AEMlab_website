Wouter Cox

Postdoc

Research Interests

I am a postdoc in the Amsterdam Emotional Memory Lab. I study the malleability of emotional memory and try to translate basic neuroscientific findings to clinical practice. In order to gain more insight in the optimal and boundary conditions of memory plasticity I conduct studies in fear conditioning paradigms as well as with human episodic memory. In a patient study I attempt to apply these fundamental insights to real-life anxiety disorders.
