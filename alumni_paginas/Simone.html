Simone Dobbelaar

Research Assistant

Research Interests

I conducted the research for my master thesis in the Emotional Memory Lab under supervision of Wouter Cox and Merel Kindt. Here, I was interested in conditions in which memories can be preserved or altered. Specifically, I focused on the role of context in declarative memory interference. I am currently assisting in the development of follow-up experiments.
