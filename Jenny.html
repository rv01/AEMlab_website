Jenny Kurath

MSc student

Research Interests

I am a Research Master student majoring in Clinical Psychology with a Minor in Psychological Methods. In the framework of my thesis project, I am currently working with Jamie Elsey on reconsolidation in fear of public speaking.

Background

I did a Bachelor in Psychology at the University of Basel where I was working as a research assistant at the Centre for Affect, Stress and Sleep, and at the Cognitive and Decision Sciences department. As part of the Research Master, I worked as an intern at the University of Western Australia on underlying mechanisms of emotional drinking.
